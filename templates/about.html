Our Journey

Resume Analyzer was created with a simple yet impactful goal: to help job seekers get past the barriers of Applicant Tracking Systems (ATS) and reach real recruiters.

Me, Harsh Koshti, and Prathamesh Gosavi, with the guidance of our mentor, HOD Dr. Sopan Talekar, saw how talented candidates were being filtered out just because their resumes weren’t ATS-friendly. We decided to change that.

What started as an idea quickly turned into a project fueled by research, testing, and constant improvements. We developed AI-driven tools that analyze resumes in real-time, providing actionable insights to boost interview chances.

We believe that job searching shouldn’t be about battling algorithms but about showcasing real skills and experience. That’s why our platform helps candidates create resumes that not only pass ATS scans but also stand out to recruiters.

Throughout our journey, we’ve worked closely with industry professionals and job seekers to refine our system. Their feedback has played a crucial role in shaping Resume Analyzer into a practical and reliable tool.

From optimizing resume formatting to suggesting relevant keywords, our AI adapts to hiring trends, ensuring that users stay ahead in the competitive job market.

Our vision goes beyond resume analysis. We aim to create a complete career support system that helps job seekers with interview preparation, skill development, and personalized career guidance.

As we continue to grow, our focus remains the same—bridging the gap between talent and opportunity while making the hiring process more transparent and fair.

- Harsh Koshti

Mission & Vision

What drives us forward and where we're headed

Our Mission

To level the playing field in the job market by ensuring that every qualified candidate has the opportunity to have their resume seen by human recruiters, regardless of their knowledge of ATS systems or resume optimization techniques.

Our Vision

We envision a future where job seekers are empowered with AI-driven tools that help them present their skills and experiences in the most effective way possible, leading to a more efficient and fair hiring process for everyone involved.

Our Core Values

The principles that guide our work

Transparency

We believe in full transparency about ATS systems and how they work. No black boxes or hidden algorithms.

Accessibility

Advanced resume optimization should be available to everyone, not just those who can afford expensive services.

Constant Innovation

We continuously improve our algorithms to stay ahead of evolving ATS technologies and industry trends.

Education

Beyond our tools, we're committed to educating job seekers about the modern hiring process.

User-Centered

Everything we build starts with understanding the real needs of job seekers in today's competitive market.

Data Privacy

We maintain the highest standards of data protection and privacy for all our users' personal information.
